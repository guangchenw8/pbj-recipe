How To: Water

Water tips and tricks

Learn how to prepare and serve water!

Ingredients
  • Bottled water
  • Tap water
  • Filtered water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200C
  2. Pour in your tap water and bottled water into a pot and set it on the stove
  3. Slowly stir the pot on high
  4. Insert a pot of filtered water into the oven
  5. Bring filtered water to a boil for 2 minutes, then immediately take it out of the oven
  6. Stir filtered water into the water mix thoroughly
  7. Allow water to cool in fridge for 5 minutes
  8. Done!
    Tip: to save leftovers for later, just store the water in the freezer, and reheat when needed!
